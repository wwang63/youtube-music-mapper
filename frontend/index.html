Upload Your Music Data

Get your YouTube Music data from Google Takeout

Option 1: Google Takeout (Recommended)

  1. Go to takeout.google.com
  2. Click "Deselect all", then select only "YouTube and YouTube Music"
  3. Under YouTube, click "All YouTube data included" and select only "music-library-songs.csv" or "playlists"
  4. Download and upload the ZIP file here

Option 2: Paste Playlist

  1. Go to your YouTube Music liked songs playlist
  2. Select all songs (Ctrl+A) and copy (Ctrl+C)
  3. Click "Paste Playlist" below
